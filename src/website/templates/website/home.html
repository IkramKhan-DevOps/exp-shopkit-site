How does Shopkit POS help me manage my inventory?

Shopkit POS allows you to track your inventory in real-time, set reorder points, and generate reports on which products are selling the most. This helps you optimize your inventory and ensure that you always have the products your customers want in stock.

Can I track customer information and purchases with Shopkit POS?

Yes, Shopkit POS includes a customer management system that allows you to store customer information, track customer purchases, and create marketing campaigns based on customer data.

Can I process different types of payments with Shopkit POS?

Yes, Shopkit POS includes support for credit cards, debit cards, and mobile payments, making it easy for your customers to pay for their purchases and for you to track payments and reconcile your sales.

Is Shopkit POS compatible with other applications?

Yes, Shopkit POS includes API's for 3D Mart applications, enabling businesses to integrate their systems with other applications, such as inventory management apps, accounting apps, and others. This allows businesses to expand their capabilities and increase their efficiency.
